MediFind is a data driven web application with category based searches about health care facilities for users who are unhappy with the lack of collective information available on health care. 

FUNCTIONALITY
1) User Login | Registration
2) User History {Record of the users interaction with the application such as appointments booked}
3) MediFind Search Function
 Parameters: categories such as clinics, pharmacy, hospitals,

Specific information returned about chosen facility 
■ Contact information 
■ Hours 
■ Appointment Scheduling 
■ Available reviews and ratings. 
■ Doctor information
